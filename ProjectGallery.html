Space Optimization Utilization:





Optimizing Truck Capacity using 2-D Binning

Company “X” wanted to find a better way of loading products into the delivery trailers that were sent to customer sites. Traditionally, forklift drivers and shipping managers had used heuristics or their best judgements to load various inventory into their 18 wheeler trucks. However, this often lead to open gaps in the cargo area due to the dynamic nature of customer demand and resulting inventory supply. The solution utilized a bin packing algorithm developed in a python environment augmented with Gurobi Optimizer. Specifically, we implemented an operation model that used the inputs a scheduler provided to determine what orders needed to go inside the trailer. From there the program provided a visual layout of the truck. This allowed the scheduler to inspect and justify the necessary operations needed to send out inventory. The last step of the process was sending the final schematic to the main computer that the forklift utilizes.
